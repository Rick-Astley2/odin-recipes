Rice Thing

What you can make in 30 minutes or less

I threw this together in 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Rice: Any type of rice will do.
  • Thai chilies: Two peppers.
  • Soy sauce: I used KIKKOMAN.
  • Kinder's Cooking Sauce and Glaze: Japanese with Soy, Ginger, and Garlic
  • Some weird thing with lemon, and lime that's brown: Make sure to look at the jar before use otherwise you will realize that it is expired by two months and eat anyway.

Directions:

  1. Cook your rice according to the bag.
  2. While rice is cooking cut up the two Thai chilies and place in bowl.
  3. Then scoop the rice in the bowl with the Thai chilies.
  4. Then add the soy sauce to the bowl, however much you want.
  5. Then add Kinder's Cooking Sauce and Glaze to the bowl.
  6. Then add some weird thing with lemon, and lime that's brown.
  7. Grab a fork and enjoy.
