Rice Thing

What you can make in 30 minutes or less

I through this together in 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Rice: any type of rice will do.

  • Thai Chilis: two peppers.

  • Soy Sauce: I used KIKKOMAN.

  • Kinder's Cooking Sauce and Glaze: Japanese with Soy, Ginger, and Garlic

  • Some weird thing with lemon, and lime thats brown: make sure to look at the jar before use other wise you will relise that it is expired by two months and eat anyway.

Directions:

  1. Cook your rice according to the bag.

  2. While rice is cooking cut up the two Thai Chilis and place in bowl.

  3. Then scoop the rice in the bowl with the Thai Chilis

  4. Then add the Soy Sauce to the bowl, how ever much you want

  5. Then add Kinder's Cooking Sauce and Glaze to the bowl

  6. Then add Some weird thing with lemon, and lime thats brown

  7. Grab a fork and enjoy
